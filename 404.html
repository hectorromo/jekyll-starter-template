The page doesn't exist

The page can't be found. Here are a couple of suggestions what could have gone wrong and how to solve it.

In the “Views”-folder?

All pages needs to be in the “Views”-folder. Put your page in the folder “Views” as pagename.php and access it without the .php.

Correct spelled?

Check the spelling both in the “Views”-folder and the url.

A sub folder in “Views”?

At the moment you can't put any sub-folders in the “Views” folder. Instead, use different names. E.g. something like offices.php together with local-office.php makes for a good, clear approach.

Something wrong with Apache?

Make sure you have enabled mod_rewrite in your Apache install. How? Let me Google that for you…
